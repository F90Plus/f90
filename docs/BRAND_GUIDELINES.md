# F90+ ‚Äî Brand Guidelines

The quick, practical guide to keeping F90+ looking like F90+: aggressive, premium,
stadium-at-night, broadcast energy, World Cup atmosphere.

## 1. The logo

**The original render is the official, primary logo** ‚Äî the gold World Cup trophy
inside the ring, chrome **"F90"**, lime **"+"**, with its glow. It is the identity.

- **File:** `apps/web/public/brand/f90plus-logo.png` (transparent).
- **Primary surfaces (use the full render):** header, hero, loading, 404, marketing,
  social. Reach for it via the `Logo` component (`src/components/layout/logo.tsx`).
- **Derived icons (tiny/system only):** favicon, app icon, maskable, avatars ‚Äî these
  are **faithful downscales of the render**, generated by
  `scripts/brand/generate-icons.py`. They are never hand-redrawn.

### Logo rules
- ‚úÖ Keep the trophy-in-circle composition, the gold, and the glow.
- ‚úÖ Place it on **dark** (night) surfaces ‚Äî that's its home.
- ‚úÖ Add the halo with the `glow` / `glow="lg"` prop on dark sections.
- ‚ùå Don't reinterpret it into a flat/minimal/abstract mark.
- ‚ùå Don't recolor, restretch, rotate, or drop the trophy.
- ‚ùå Don't place it on a busy or light background without a dark container.

### Clear space & sizing
- **Clear space:** keep padding ‚â• 20% of the logo height on all sides.
- **Min sizes:** header ‚âà 36‚Äì40px tall ¬∑ favicon derived (don't render the full
  render below ~28px ‚Äî use the icon badge).
- The render is 500√ó500 (‚âàsquare); it scales cleanly down to header size and up to
  ~460px (hero/OG). For larger marketing, request a higher-res export.

## 3. Glow language

Glow = stadium lights + broadcast. Keep it **soft and purposeful**, never neon-spam.

- **Logo halo:** `.logo-glow` (default) / `.logo-glow-lg` (hero, loading) ‚Äî gold +
  lime drop-shadow.
- **Surfaces:** `.glow-led` / `.glow-lime` / `.glow-gold` for elevated cards/CTAs.
- **Ambient:** the hero `StadiumBackdrop` (drifting LED/volt/pitch/gold blurs).
- **Intensity guide:** hero/loading = strong; cards/CTAs = medium; everywhere else =
  subtle or none. If two glows touch, dial one back.

## 4. Typography

- **Display / headlines ‚Üí Space Grotesk** (700‚Äì800). `h1`‚Äì`h3`, big numerals,
  uppercase broadcast labels.
- **UI / body ‚Üí Geist Sans.** Clean, legible, premium.
- **Numerals ‚Üí tabular** (`.nums`) for scores, points, countdowns.
- **Eyebrow** ‚Üí `.eyebrow` (uppercase, wide tracking, LED-blue) ‚Äî the signature
  lower-third label.
- Headlines are **tight** (`tracking-tight`, `text-balance`); body is comfortable.

## 6. Voice & atmosphere (visual)

Aggressive, premium, modern, sporty, international. Think **World Cup night under
the floodlights, shot like a broadcast** ‚Äî never casino, never generic, never flat.
Movement is confident (one ease-out curve); everything feels alive and tactile.

## Don'ts (quick list)
‚ùå Casino styling ¬∑ ‚ùå neon overload ¬∑ ‚ùå flat/minimal reinterpretations of the logo ¬∑
‚ùå light/busy backgrounds for the logo without a dark container ¬∑ ‚ùå raw hex in
components ¬∑ ‚ùå stretching or recoloring the render ¬∑ ‚ùå generic stock-sportsbook UI.
