# F90+ — Project Vision

## In one line

**F90+ is the social, free-to-play prediction experience for the 2026 World Cup** —
where calling matches with friends turns every game into a moment, with virtual
coins and global bragging rights instead of money.

## The name

**F90+** → *Fútbol* + the **90** minutes of a match + the **“+”** for everything
extra: extra time, the social layer, the moments beyond the scoreline.

## What it is

A place to:

- **Predict** match winners, scorelines and big moments before kickoff.
- **Earn virtual coins** for good calls and spend them on bolder predictions.
- **Climb a global leaderboard** that moves with the tournament.
- **Compete with friends** in private leagues and group chats.
- **Spar with a football-smart AI pundit** that argues from stats, not money.

## What it is **not**

- ❌ **Not real-money betting.** No deposits, no withdrawals, no cash prizes.
- ❌ **Not a sportsbook.** No odds-as-payouts, no house edge, no aggressive
  casino patterns. Probabilities are a *signal*, never a wager.
- ❌ **Not pay-to-win.** Everything core is free. Monetization, if any, never
  buys predictive advantage.

This distinction is a **product principle**, not just legal hygiene. It shapes
the tone, the visuals (stadium, not casino) and every future feature.

## Who it's for

- **The World Cup superfan** who already predicts every match out loud.
- **The friend group** that wants a shared scoreboard for the tournament.
- **The casual viewer** pulled in by a global event, who wants skin in the game
  without risking a cent.

Global from the start — which is why the product ships **bilingual (ES / EN)**
and is architected to add languages cheaply.

## Experience principles

1. **It should feel like World Cup night.** Premium, broadcast-grade, alive —
   never a boring dashboard or an ugly sportsbook.
2. **Free and honest.** Virtual-only, transparent, no dark patterns.
3. **Social before solo.** The fun compounds with friends; the product leans
   into leagues, sharing and friendly rivalry.
4. **Fast and tactile.** Making a call is one confident tap with satisfying
   feedback.
5. **Smart, not preachy.** The AI pundit adds insight and banter, not lectures.

## What success looks like

- People choose F90+ as their **default second screen** during matches.
- Predictions are **shared** — a friend invite is the main growth loop.
- The experience feels **premium enough to be mistaken for a paid app**, while
  being free.

## Current phase

**Foundation** — visual identity, design system, i18n, and a homepage that
already transmits the energy. No accounts, predictions, wallet, leaderboard
persistence, AI or live data yet. The roadmap to those lives in
[`ROADMAP.md`](ROADMAP.md).
