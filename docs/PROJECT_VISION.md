# F90+ — Project Vision (canonical north star)

> The definitive product vision. If a feature doesn't serve this, it doesn't ship.

## In one line
**F90+ is the living experience of the 2026 World Cup** — you don't just watch the
tournament, you *live* it: predict, build your team, trade players, follow the world's
pulse, compete globally, and watch your own story evolve in real time. Free, virtual,
premium. The World Cup as one world — and that world is yours to play.

## The name
**F90+** → *Fútbol* + the **90** minutes + the **"+"** for everything extra: extra
time, the social layer, the story beyond the scoreline.

## The thesis (what makes F90+ different)
Everyone else picks **one** mechanic — a fantasy game, a betting app, a prediction
market, a tracker. **F90+ fuses them into one living experience.** The magic isn't
having predictions *and* fantasy *and* markets; it's that they're **the same story**:

> The globe isn't decoration. Reputation isn't just a ranking. Predictions aren't an
> isolated tab. Fantasy isn't a separate mode. The market isn't a standalone mechanic.
> **Everything feeds the same living story of the user during the World Cup.**

## The spine — four shared layers under everything
Every pillar plugs into the same four layers. This is what stops F90+ from being a
"mix of features" (full detail in [EXPERIENCE_SYSTEM.md](EXPERIENCE_SYSTEM.md)):

1. **One world** — the **globe**: the official World Cup, alive, in real time. The map
   of the whole experience and the planet's football pulse.
2. **One identity** — your **public profile & reputation**: your story, your record,
   your country. Every action builds you as a character.
3. **One economy** — **virtual coins + points/reputation**: the shared currency earned
   and spent across *all* mechanics. No real money, ever.
4. **One timeline** — everything moves **live with the real tournament**: each real
   match recomputes your predictions, portfolio, fantasy, ranking and reputation.

## The pillars (what you do — all feeding the spine)
- **Predict** matches, scorelines and moments.
- **Build your ideal XI** (fantasy) and run it through the tournament.
- **Discover & trade players** — a smart **portfolio**: find sleepers, sign early,
  back the undervalued before they explode (strategic, *not* an arcade pack-opener).
- **Follow the markets** — sentiment and movement, as signal (never a wager).
- **Climb global rankings** and build **reputation**.
- **Live the narrative** — an editorial/AI layer that *interprets* the World Cup.
- **Belong to your country** — country hubs, community, national story.
- **Feel the world's pulse** — momentum/heat: hottest nations, trending players,
  global predictions, world sentiment, moving in real time.
- **Track it live** — the whole experience updates as the tournament happens.

## What it is **not**
- ❌ **Not real-money betting / a sportsbook.** No deposits, withdrawals, cash prizes,
  odds-as-payouts or house edge. Probabilities are a *signal*, never a wager.
- ❌ **Not pay-to-win.** Everything core is free; monetization never buys predictive edge.
- ❌ **Not an arcade FUT/Panini/EA clone.** Discovery & cards are strategic and
  original — our own visual identity, **no licensed assets** (D-025).
- ❌ **Not a cold tracker.** It's *narrated and lived*, not just dashboards and data.
- ❌ **Not built on invented data.** Every state/number comes from **verified real
  data** — no fabrication, no fake states (the globe's rule, generalized — D-023).

## Experience principles
1. **It feels like World Cup night.** Premium, broadcast-grade, cinematic, alive —
   never a boring dashboard or an ugly sportsbook.
2. **One living story.** Pillars share world + identity + economy + time; the user is
   always building *one* evolving story.
3. **Real or nothing.** Everything is driven by verified real data; we never invent
   states, stats or outcomes.
4. **Narrated, not just tracked.** A premium editorial/AI voice gives the tournament
   soul ("Croatia is surviving", "Japan is the tactical surprise").
5. **Always playable — never punitive.** If your country is eliminated you keep your
   account and every mechanic; only the *emotional narrative* shifts. The World Cup
   stays alive for everyone until the final (D-026).
6. **Social & tribal.** Country identity, hubs, leagues, rivalry — the World Cup is
   global belonging.
7. **Own identity, no fragile licenses.** Original F90+ cards, stylized avatars, our
   own iconography — a recognizable premium language, not a copyright liability (D-025).
8. **Free and honest.** Virtual-only, transparent, no dark patterns.
9. **Mobile + desktop, equally premium** (dual-surface parity — D-021); tool-agnostic
   and portable (D-016); product in **English**, team in **Spanish**.

## Who it's for
The superfan who predicts every match out loud · the friend group that wants a shared
World Cup · the casual viewer pulled in by a global event who wants skin in the game
without risking a cent · the strategist who loves spotting talent early. Global from day
one — bilingual (ES/EN), architected to add languages cheaply.

## What success looks like
F90+ becomes the **default second screen** of the World Cup; sharing your story / inviting
friends is the main growth loop; it feels **premium enough to be mistaken for a paid app**
while being free; and it feels **alive** — like the tournament is happening *inside* it.

## How we build it
The **vision is documented in full now**; the **build goes in coherent slices**
(*premium-or-don't-ship*, *fewer-but-excellent* — D-020). The full experience is the
north; **Phase 1 selects the first playable MVP slice**. Order before deep build
(founder): canonical vision → definitive hero (production-ready globe) → connected
domain → metadata/social/loading/performance → strong Phase 1 definition.
